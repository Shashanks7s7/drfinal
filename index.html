Us
About Us
Meet our team of four Computer Engineering students from Cosmos Engineering College. We're working on our final project, which focuses on the 'Detection of Diabetic Retinopathy using Deep Learning.' Our goal is to leverage AI technology to improve diabetic retinopathy diagnosis and contribute to better patient care. With dedication and enthusiasm, we're excited to make a positive impact in the field of healthcare. Together, we strive to create a meaningful solution and enhance the early detection of diabetic retinopathy.
Mission
Objectives
The objectives of this project are threefold. First, we aim to detect diabetic retinopathy (DR) with higher accuracy and screen patients more rapidly compared to conventional diagnosis systems. Secondly, our goal is to develop an economical and faster detection technique for diabetic retinopathy using deep learning. Lastly, we seek to enhance the accuracy of pre-existing low accuracy detection techniques by leveraging the power of effective deep learning algorithms and transfer learning.
